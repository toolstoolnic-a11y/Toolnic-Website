Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Toolnic.

Toolnic is an all-in-one Android utility app that combines 50+ powerful tools into a single, beautifully designed experience. From AI summarization to PDF editing, speed testing to invoice creation it's the ultimate digital toolbox.
Yes, Toolnic is free to download and use. We believe powerful tools should be accessible to everyone. Some advanced AI features may have usage limits, but the core experience is completely free.
Many tools work completely offline, including the compass, flashlight, QR scanner, calculators, converters, and PDF reader. AI features and speed tests require an internet connection.
Upload any PDF document to Toolnic, and our AI engine will analyze the content, extract key information, and generate a concise summary. It also supports read-aloud functionality with natural voices.
Absolutely. Toolnic is built with privacy as a core principle. We don't collect unnecessary data, and all processing is done securely. Your documents and personal information never leave your device without your explicit consent.
Yes! Toolnic integrates with Android's file system. Simply tap any PDF in your file manager and choose Toolnic as the app to open it. The PDF reader will launch instantly with full functionality.
Toolnic supports 20+ languages for the UI, AI summaries, and text-to-speech. The app automatically detects your device language and adapts accordingly.
We release updates every 2-4 weeks with new tools, improvements, and bug fixes. We're constantly adding new features based on user feedback.
Toolnic is ad-supported but we keep ads non-intrusive and respectful of your experience. No full-screen pop-ups or interruptive ads. We believe in putting the user first.
Toolnic only asks for permissions it actually needs storage access for documents, camera for QR scanning, and microphone for voice reminders. No unnecessary permissions are requested.
Yes! Toolnic is fully optimized for tablets with a responsive layout that adapts to larger screens. The experience is just as polished on tablets as on phones.
Yes, Toolnic features a beautiful dark mode that's easy on the eyes. It automatically follows your system theme, or you can toggle it manually in settings.
You can reach our support team via email at support@toolnic.app, or through the in-app feedback system. We typically respond within 24 hours.
Currently, Toolnic is available exclusively on Android. We're exploring an iOS version for the future.
