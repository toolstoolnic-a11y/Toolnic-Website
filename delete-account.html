What Account Data Is Deleted

When your account deletion request is processed, the following account-related information will be permanently removed from our systems:

Google Sign-In association
Authentication records

This action is permanent and irreversible. Once your account is deleted, you will not be able to recover it or any associated data tied to your account on our servers.

Local Data Stored on Your Device

Settings
You can delete this local data at any time
Clear history from within the app using the in-app clear options.
Clear app storage from Android Settings → Apps → Toolnic → Storage.
Uninstall the Toolnic app to remove all locally stored data.

Data Retention Policy

Toolnic does not permanently store your locally saved history on our servers. If any authentication-related account information exists, it will be permanently deleted after your request is verified.

30
Days Maximum Retention Period Any authentication-related account information will be permanently and irreversibly deleted within 30 days after your deletion request has been verified by our team.
