Information We Collect

What data Toolnic may receive

Toolnic is designed to collect only the information necessary to provide its services.

If you choose to sign in using Google Sign-In, we may receive your name, email address, profile picture (if available), and a unique Google account identifier. This information is used solely for authentication and account identification.

We never collect or store your Google account password.

You may voluntarily provide information such as documents selected for processing, reminder details, preferences, feedback, or support requests.

To improve app performance and reliability, Toolnic may automatically collect limited technical information including your device model, OS version, app version, language, time zone, and anonymous crash diagnostics.

Toolnic requests only the permissions required for the features you choose to use:

Photos & Files

Used for opening, editing, converting, compressing, saving, and managing files you select.

Notifications

Internet Access

Required for Google Sign-In, AI-powered features, advertisement services, and app updates.
